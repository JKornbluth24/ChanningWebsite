Our Strategy

Multi-tenant commercial assets. Conservative structures.

Channing targets income-producing, multi-tenant commercial real estate in select markets with the intention of providing investors with consistent, predictable returns comprised of current income and capital appreciation.

I
Current Income

Solid, consistent, and predictable cash flow is a key feature of real estate investing. Current income that may be tax advantaged reduces the dependence on residual values to generate overall investor returns. Channing targets assets capable of producing an average 9% current yield to investors.

II
Debt Reduction

Matching an asset's duration with the maturity of its liabilities is an important corporate finance discipline. Reducing outstanding debt balances further reduces risk and creates the opportunity for significant cash flow events upon capital transactions.

III
Asset Appreciation

Acquiring good real estate in strong locations, effectively managing the physical asset, and taking care of tenants should over time result in increases in asset value and more equity proceeds upon an exit or refinancing.

◇
Non-Correlation

Privately held real estate returns are less correlated to the broader public market than publicly traded REITs, offering genuine portfolio diversification and materially lower volatility compared to publicly traded stocks and bonds.

◈
Inflation Protection

Real estate is an effective inflation hedge given hard asset protection and the potential for compensating increases in net operating income as leases reset to market rates over time.

◉
Structural Downside Protection

The three value drivers work in concert: if one falters, the others provide downside protection. This resilience is a key reason direct real estate investing remains among the best asset classes for long-term wealth creation.

01
Income-Producing Assets

We focus exclusively on assets generating current income at acquisition or with a clear near-term path to stabilization. Speculative development or land plays are outside our mandate.

02
Multi-Tenant Structure

Multi-tenant properties reduce single-tenant risk and provide diversified income streams. This structure aligns with our conservative approach to capital preservation and income stability.

03
Select Market Positioning

We target assets in markets with demonstrated demand drivers, favorable supply dynamics, and long-term economic fundamentals that support tenant retention and rental growth.

04
Conservative Capital Structure

We avoid overleveraged deals. Conservative loan-to-value ratios and matching of debt maturity to asset duration are non-negotiable criteria in our underwriting process.

05
Value-Add Potential

We favor assets where active management — leasing, capital improvement, operational efficiency — can meaningfully enhance value beyond what the going-in yield already reflects.

06
Aligned Transaction Structure

Channing seeks transactions where seller motivation, pricing, and deal structure are aligned with our investment objectives — enabling fair outcomes for all parties and strong returns for investors.
